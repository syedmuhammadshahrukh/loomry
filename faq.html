Pricing & MOQ

MOQs vary by product and quality tier:

  • T-Shirts: 150 pieces per style/color (Tier 1-2), 500 pieces (Tier 3)
  • Hoodies: 100 pieces per style/color (Tier 1-2), 500 pieces (Tier 3)
  • Sweatshirts: 100 pieces per style/color (Tier 1-2), 500 pieces (Tier 3)

These quantities are per style/color combination. For example, if you want Black and White hoodies, that's 100 Black + 100 White = 200 total minimum.

Quality & Materials

Our three tiers are designed for different brand positions:

  • Tier 1 (Budget) ‚Äî Good quality basics. 180-200 GSM for tees, cotton-poly blends. Great for startups testing the market or merch.
  • Tier 2 (Core) ‚Äî Retail-ready quality. 200-240 GSM ringspun cotton, side-seamed construction, custom labels included. Competes with mall brands.
  • Tier 3 (Premium) ‚Äî Luxury streetwear grade. 240-420 GSM heavyweight fabrics, flatlock seams, custom knit options. Competes with designer labels.

Not sure which tier? Order samples from each tier to feel the difference.

GSM = Grams per Square Meter. It measures fabric weight/thickness.

  • 140-180 GSM ‚Äî Lightweight, thin tees (promotional quality)
  • 180-220 GSM ‚Äî Standard t-shirt weight (everyday retail)
  • 220-260 GSM ‚Äî Premium heavyweight tees (luxury feel)
  • 280-350 GSM ‚Äî Standard fleece/hoodies
  • 350-420 GSM ‚Äî Heavyweight luxury fleece

Higher GSM = heavier, more substantial feel. Try our fabric weight guide to explore.

We offer multiple decoration methods:

  • Screen Printing ‚Äî Best for bulk orders, bold colors, durability. 1-4 colors most economical.
  • DTG (Direct-to-Garment) ‚Äî Best for complex designs, photos, gradients. No color limits.
  • Embroidery ‚Äî Premium look for logos. Tier 2-3 recommended for best results.
  • Heat Transfer ‚Äî Good for small runs, all-over prints.

We'll recommend the best method based on your design, quantity, and budget.

Shipping & Delivery

Worldwide. We ship to all major markets including:

  • United States & Canada
  • European Union & UK
  • Australia & New Zealand
  • Middle East & Asia

Shipping costs and times vary by destination. We provide full shipping quotes before you commit.

We offer multiple shipping options:

  • Air Freight ‚Äî 7-14 days, best for urgent/smaller orders
  • Sea Freight ‚Äî 4-6 weeks, most economical for large orders
  • Express Courier ‚Äî 3-5 days (DHL, FedEx), premium cost

For orders over 500 units, sea freight typically makes the most economic sense.

Yes, import duties are the buyer's responsibility. These vary by country:

  • USA: Usually 12-20% on apparel (may be exempt under certain thresholds)
  • EU: ~12% duty + local VAT
  • UK: ~12% duty + 20% VAT
  • Australia: 5% duty + 10% GST

We can ship DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) if you prefer ‚Äî duties included in your quote upfront.

We focus on bulk manufacturing, not dropshipping.

However, we can:

  • Ship bulk orders to your 3PL/fulfillment center
  • Split shipments to multiple warehouse locations
  • Recommend fulfillment partners we work with

For single-unit fulfillment, you'll need a 3PL partner to handle individual orders.
