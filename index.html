Quality Tiers

Choose your quality level

Three distinct tiers to match every brand's stage and positioning.

Tier 1

Budget

Best for: Test drops, influencer samples, market validation

  • Cotton/Poly blend (70/30)
  • 280–300 GSM (T-shirts: 180–200)
  • Single needle stitching
  • Screen print branding
  • Normal wash finish
  • ±5% tolerance

Minimum Order

100 pcs

per color, per style

Tier 2 — Most Popular

Core

Best for: Retail-ready brands, scaling businesses

  • 100% combed cotton
  • 320–350 GSM (T-shirts: 210–240)
  • Double stitching + reinforced seams
  • Puff/HD print or embroidery
  • Enzyme + silicon wash, pre-shrunk
  • ±3% tolerance

Minimum Order

200 pcs

per color

Tier 3

Premium

Best for: Luxury positioning, premium retail

  • Custom knit cotton / compact cotton
  • 380–420 GSM (T-shirts: 240–260)
  • Flatlock / coverstitch + neck taping
  • Embroidery only + custom trims
  • Full finishing with bio polish
  • ±2% tolerance

Minimum Order

500 pcs

per style
